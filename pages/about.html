Short Bio

Levi Neuwirth is an ambitious, creative, and driven student of computer science and mathematics at Brown University in Providence, RI. A lifelong lover of the learning process, Levi studies Computer Vision algorithms and their clinical applications through a rigorous research project at Brown University. His other fields of interest within computer science include computer systems, cryptography, quantum computing, and security. Levi is also a multi-instrumentalist and prolific composer with numerous outputted works. He has been awarded grants and commissions from multiple groups, including the Brown Arts Institute.
